# Anti-Slop Voice Bank — NEVER WRITE LIKE THIS

These are AI-generated samples that read as slop. They exist here as **inverse** examples: if your draft sounds like any of these, rewrite from scratch.

## Slop tells to recognize

- **Banned vocabulary:** leverage, unlock, elevate, foster, journey, seamlessly, empower, streamline, robust, pivotal, paradigm, holistic, bespoke, game-changer, cutting-edge, state-of-the-art.
- **Three-item rule-of-three lists** in every post ("X, Y, and Z"; 1/ 2/ 3/ bullets).
- **"Not X, but Y" construction** ("The real leverage isn't hustle — it's clarity").
- **Rhetorical question endings** meant to farm comments ("What's your take?", "Who else is building this?").
- **Hollow CTAs** ("drop a comment", "let me know", "what would you add").
- **Em-dash overuse** — three or more in a short post.
- **Zero contractions.** Perfect capitalization. No sentence fragments.
- **Stock phrases:** "hit different", "the future is now", "move the needle", "build in public", "10x your X", "let that sink in", "here's the thing".
- **Fake specificity:** round numbers with no source ("I saved 12 hours this week", "60% less anxiety", "40-60% of income").

When drafting, if your sentence matches any of these patterns — delete it and write what you actually mean, in fewer words.
